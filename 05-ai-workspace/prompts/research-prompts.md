# Research Prompts — AI-Assisted University & Career Research

> Use these prompts to accelerate research on programs, careers, and the US market.

---

## University Research

### Prompt R1 — Deep Program Analysis
```
Analyze the [PROGRAM NAME] at [UNIVERSITY NAME] for a candidate with this profile:

Background: MMS Marketing (India, 9.4 CGPA), Brand Manager (1 year), 5 marketing internships including Deloitte and Godrej, sociology/anthropology BA
Skill gaps: SQL, Python, statistics
Career goal: Marketing Analyst or Consumer Insights Analyst in US tech or CPG

Provide:
1. How well this program matches the profile (score /10 with reasoning)
2. Top 5 most relevant courses for this candidate
3. Typical career outcomes and hiring companies
4. Any red flags or mismatches
5. Key selling points to mention in the SOP for this school specifically
```

### Prompt R2 — Alumni Career Mapping
```
Based on publicly available information, what are the typical career paths for graduates of [PROGRAM] at [SCHOOL]?

I am specifically interested in:
- International students (F-1 visa / OPT)
- Those who came from marketing backgrounds (not technical/engineering)
- Roles in marketing analytics, consumer insights, brand strategy

List 5–10 realistic job titles and companies that recruit from this program.
```

### Prompt R3 — Program Comparison
```
Compare these two programs for a marketing professional transitioning to analytics:

Program A: MS in Marketing Analytics, Simon Business School (Rochester)
Program B: MS in Marketing Analytics and Insights, Rutgers Business School

Candidate profile: Indian national, MMS Marketing, brand management background, targeting marketing analyst roles in NYC/US

Compare on:
1. Curriculum rigor and analytics depth
2. Career outcomes for marketing backgrounds
3. Location advantage for job search
4. STEM OPT implications
5. Cost vs ROI
6. Overall recommendation
```

## Career Research

### Prompt R4 — Job Role Deep Dive
```
Describe the day-to-day work of a [JOB TITLE] at a US [tech / CPG / consulting] company.

Specifically:
1. What does the role actually do week-to-week?
2. What tools and skills are used daily?
3. What does strong performance look like?
4. What is the typical career ladder after this role?
5. What does the interview process look like?
6. For an international student on OPT, what should I highlight in applications?
```

### Prompt R5 — Resume Bullet Rewriter (Analytics Lens)
```
Rewrite these resume bullets to emphasize the data and analytical aspects for a marketing analytics program application:

Original bullets:
[PASTE BULLETS]

Make them more quantified, analytical, and oriented toward data-driven decision making.
Keep each bullet under 2 lines. Use strong action verbs.
```

### Prompt R6 — US Job Market Intelligence
```
What are the top 10 companies that hire marketing analysts / consumer insights analysts in the US (especially NYC and surrounding areas)?

For each company provide:
1. What the marketing analytics team does
2. Typical entry-level roles for new MS graduates
3. H-1B sponsorship history
4. How to get in (recruiting channels, campus presence)
```

## Scholarship Research

```
List all scholarships, fellowships, and funding opportunities available to Indian students pursuing MS programs in Marketing Analytics or Management Analytics at US universities (specifically Rochester, Rutgers, NYU, CUNY Baruch) starting January 2027.

Include:
- University-specific scholarships
- External scholarships open to Indian nationals
```
