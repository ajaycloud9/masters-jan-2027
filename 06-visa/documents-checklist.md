# F-1 Visa Documents Checklist

> Complete this section once you have an I-20 in hand.

---

## Pre-Visa Application Steps

| Step | Action | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Receive I-20 from university | ⬜ | Requires enrollment deposit |
| 2 | Pay SEVIS fee ($350) at fmjfee.com | ⬜ | Pay before DS-160 |
| 3 | Complete DS-160 online application | ⬜ | Save confirmation number |
| 4 | Schedule visa appointment (Mumbai consulate) | ⬜ | Book early — slots fill fast |
| 5 | Pay MRV (visa) fee | ⬜ | Currently $185 USD |
