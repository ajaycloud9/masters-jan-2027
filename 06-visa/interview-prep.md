# F-1 Visa Interview Preparation

> Build this section after receiving at least one admission offer and I-20.  
> The visa interview is the final gate — preparation here is as important as the application itself.

---

## Key Things the Consular Officer is Evaluating

1. **Genuine student intent** — Are you going to study, not immigrate permanently?
2. **Ties to home country** — Will you return to India after studies?
3. **Financial capacity** — Can you pay for the program without becoming a burden?
4. **Academic fit** — Is this program logical given your background?
5. **Clear career plan** — Do you have a credible post-study plan?

## Interview Day Checklist

### Documents to Carry (Originals + Copies)
- [ ] Valid passport (+ old passports if any)
- [ ] DS-160 confirmation page
- [ ] Visa appointment confirmation
- [ ] I-20 (original, signed by DSO)
- [ ] SEVIS fee receipt (Form I-901)
- [ ] Admission letter from university
- [ ] Financial documents (bank statements, sponsor letter, scholarship letter)
- [ ] Academic transcripts (all degrees)
- [ ] Resume / CV
- [ ] GRE/GMAT score report (if applicable)
- [ ] Proof of ties to India (family, property, employment offer post-study)

### Day-Before Prep
- [ ] Print all documents — 2 copies of each
- [ ] Organize in a clean folder by category
- [ ] Review top 10 most likely questions
- [ ] Get 8 hours of sleep
- [ ] Dress professionally (business casual minimum)
