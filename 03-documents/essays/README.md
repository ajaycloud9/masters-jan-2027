# Essays

Place all school-specific short-answer essays and supplemental writing here.

## Format

Create a subfolder per school as needed:
```
essays/
  NYU/
  Rochester/
  Rutgers/
  CUNY/
```

## Common Essay Prompts to Prepare For

| Prompt Type | Example | Schools |
|---|---|---|
| Career goals | "What are your short and long-term career goals?" | Most schools |
| Why this program | "Why are you applying to this specific program?" | Most schools |
| Leadership experience | "Describe a time you led a team through a challenge." | Some schools |
| Diversity / contribution | "What unique perspective do you bring to our community?" | Some schools |
| Failure / resilience | "Describe a professional setback and what you learned." | Some schools |
| Optional essay | "Is there anything else you'd like us to know?" | Most schools |

## Tips
- Keep responses tightly aligned to SOP themes (same narrative, different depth)
- Word limits are strict — draft long, then cut aggressively
- Specific > General. Always use a real example over a general statement.
