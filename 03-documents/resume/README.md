## Tailoring Guidelines

### For Analytics-heavy programs (NYU, Rochester, Rutgers)
- Lead with quantified impact in every bullet
- Add any SQL/Python/analytics coursework or projects as a skills section
- Reframe ICICI Securities internship to highlight the 90+ UX data audit (analytical lens)
- Highlight the market research and data-driven elements from all internships

### For Marketing-heavy programs (CUNY)
- Lead with brand management and campaign strategy experience
- Emphasize creative and strategic work at Anika Parekh Consultancy
- Highlight leadership: 150+ campaigns, committee coordinator

## Format Requirements
- **Length:** 1 page maximum (US standard for <5 years experience)
- **Format:** PDF for submission, .md or .docx for editing
- **Font:** Clean, professional (Calibri, Arial, or similar)
- **Margins:** 0.5–0.75 inch
