**Division of Godrej & Boyce Mfg. Co. Ltd.**

India's oldest (since 1902) and largest manufacturer and marketer of security solutions. The division covers:

- **Electronic security:** CCTV cameras, video door phones, electronic safes, access control systems, alarm systems
- **Smart/digital products:** Biometric safes with LED touchpads, auto-freeze options, wireless alarm systems (e.g., "Eagle-I-Lite"), portable personal lockers (e.g., "Goldilocks" at ₹6,499)

**Scale and reach:** Exports to 55+ countries across Middle East, South East Asia, Far East Asia, East Africa, US, Europe, and SAARC. Serves prestigious clients including SBI, PNB, Muthoot, Taj Mahal monument, Qutub Minar, Ajanta-Ellora caves, Mumbai Trans Harbour Link (Atal Setu), and ISRO's Satish Dhawan Space Centre.

**Recognition:** Awarded Superbrand and Powerbrand status. "Most Preferred brand" in the Home Segment. "Security Company of the Year" at BW Securityworld Conclave and Excellence Awards 2023. Revenue target: ₹1,500 crore by FY27, with overseas share growing from 10% to 15%.
