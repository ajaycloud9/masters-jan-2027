## Strategic Framing

Each LOR should cover a **different dimension** of Shradha's profile. Adcoms want a 3D picture — not two recommenders saying the same thing.

| Recommender | Ideal Relationship | What They Should Cover |
|---|---|---|
| Recommender 1 | Professional supervisor (Brand Manager / Deloitte) | Leadership, strategic thinking, impact under pressure, initiative |
| Recommender 2 | Academic (SIMSREE faculty) or senior internship mentor | Analytical growth, intellectual curiosity, program readiness |

## Briefing Packet Contents (for each recommender)
Share these with every recommender:
1. Updated resume
2. SOP first draft (so their letter complements, not duplicates)
3. 3–5 specific stories/examples they can reference
4. List of schools + deadlines + submission portal links
5. Talking points document (what you'd like them to emphasize)
