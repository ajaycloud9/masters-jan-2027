### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
**Used in bullets:** Anika Parekh (campaign referral traffic), Godrej Interio (page-level audit), ICICI Securities (email cohort classification), Joveo (channel attribution)
**Why it matters:** Appears in 4 out of 6 roles. It is the single most credible analytics tool you can claim for a marketing analytics programme because it bridges campaign performance and web behaviour data — exactly what NYU's Data-Driven Decision Making course covers.
**How to learn it:**
- Google's own free certification: **Google Analytics Certification** (via Skillshop) — 4–6 hours
- Practice: Connect GA4 to a free website (GitHub Pages or Blogger) and set up events, goals, and audience segments
- Project idea: Analyse 3 months of traffic data from a blog or personal site; build a Looker Studio report on top of it
**Target level:** Comfortable navigating Explore reports, setting up conversion events, building audience segments, reading channel attribution
**Timeline:** 2 weeks | **Cost:** Free

---

### Google Looker Studio
**Used in bullets:** Anika Parekh (campaign KPI dashboard), Godrej Interio (activation live dashboard)
**Why it matters:** This is the tool that makes your dashboard-building work verifiable. It connects directly to GA4, Google Sheets, and Meta Ads — the exact sources named in your Anika Parekh bullets.
**How to learn it:**
- Google's free Looker Studio course on Skillshop — 2 hours
- Practice: Connect Looker Studio to a GA4 property and a Google Sheet; build a 5-metric marketing dashboard
- Project idea: Build a mock campaign dashboard using sample data (Google provides public datasets)
**Target level:** Build multi-source dashboards with filters, date controls, and calculated fields
**Timeline:** 1 week after GA4 | **Cost:** Free

---

### Screaming Frog SEO Spider
**Used in bullets:** Godrej Interio (200+ asset audit), ICICI Securities (90+ page crawl)
**Why it matters:** This is the credibility tool for your two audit bullets. It is free up to 500 URLs, takes 30 minutes to learn, and immediately makes the "I audited 200+ assets" claim technically specific rather than vague.
**How to learn it:**
- Download Screaming Frog (free tier, up to 500 URLs): screaming frog.co.uk
- Tutorial: Ahrefs and Moz both have free Screaming Frog walkthrough videos on YouTube — under 2 hours total
- Practice: Crawl your LinkedIn profile URL or any public website; export the metadata report to Excel and identify 5 issues
**Target level:** Run a crawl, export metadata fields, identify SEO issues, filter by status code and content type
**Timeline:** 3 days | **Cost:** Free (up to 500 URLs)

---

### Google Search Console
**Used in bullets:** ICICI Securities (SEO validation), Joveo (keyword ranking cross-reference)
**Why it matters:** The two SEO bullets use SEMrush and Search Console as a pair — SEMrush for competitive data, Search Console for actual performance data. Without Search Console knowledge, the pairing sounds made up in an interview.
**How to learn it:**
- Connect your own Gmail account to a free Google Search Console property (you can use any website you own or contribute to)
- Google's official Search Console training course — free, 3 hours
- Practice: Pull the Performance report for a website; analyse impressions vs. clicks vs. average position by query
**Target level:** Read and interpret Performance reports; understand impressions, clicks, CTR, and average position by query
**Timeline:** 1 week | **Cost:** Free

---
